# Deka Global Champions Fund - Detailed Holdings Analysis

## Fund Structure Overview

**Fund Type**: Equity Fund (Aktienfonds)
**Investment Focus**: Global Champions Strategy
**Benchmark**: MSCI World Index
**Currency**: Euro (EUR)
**Inception**: Multi-decade track record

## Investment Philosophy

The Deka Global Champions fund follows a quality-growth investment approach that seeks companies with:

1. **Market Leadership**: Dominant positions in their industries
2. **Competitive Moats**: Sustainable competitive advantages
3. **Strong Financials**: Consistent profitability and cash flow generation
4. **Growth Potential**: Ability to grow revenues and earnings over time
5. **ESG Considerations**: Environmental, Social, and Governance factors

## Detailed Sector Analysis

### Technology Sector (30.88%)

The fund's largest allocation is to technology companies, reflecting the digital transformation of the global economy.

**Key Holdings**:
- NVIDIA (6.55%): AI and graphics processing leader
- Microsoft (6.33%): Cloud computing and enterprise software
- Alphabet/Google (6.23%): Search, advertising, and cloud services
- Broadcom (3.57%): Semiconductors and infrastructure software
- Taiwan Semiconductor (1.96%): World's largest chip manufacturer

**Why This Matters**:
Technology companies offer high growth potential but also carry concentration risk. The fund is overweight technology compared to category average (30.88% vs 27.04%).

### Communication Services (14.12%)

Significantly overweight compared to category average (8.51%).

**Key Component**:
- Meta Platforms (4.35%): Social media and metaverse investments

**Rationale**: Digital communication and social media platforms have become essential infrastructure for modern society.

### Consumer Cyclical (12.94%)

Above category average (9.21%).

**Key Holdings**:
- Amazon (5.37%): E-commerce and cloud computing

**Characteristics**: These companies benefit from consumer spending and economic growth but may underperform during recessions.

### Financial Services (11.98%)

Underweight compared to category average (15.93%).

**Key Holdings**:
- JPMorgan Chase (2.15%): Largest US bank by assets
- Mastercard (2.19%): Payment processing network

**Strategy**: Selective exposure to high-quality financial institutions with strong competitive positions.

### Healthcare (9.86%)

In line with category average (10.29%).

**Characteristics**: Defensive sector providing stability and dividend income.

### Energy (5.00%)

Significantly overweight category average (1.92%).

**Rationale**: Recent addition likely reflects energy sector recovery and dividend opportunities.
