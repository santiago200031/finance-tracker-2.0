## Investment Strategy

The Deka Global Champions fund is an open-ended equity fund that primarily invests in a globally diversified portfolio of stocks. The fund follows a "Champion Strategy" focusing on companies with:

- Leading market positions within their respective industries
- Significant growth potential
- Strong competitive advantages
- Sustainable business models

The fund aims to provide investors with long-term capital appreciation through investments in high-quality global companies.
